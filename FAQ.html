What exactly is A Touch of Home?

A Touch of Home stands as a distinguished senior living retirement community tailored for discerning older adults in North Austin, Texas. Above all, it offers an enriching residential retirement choice that promotes health and wellness through an engaging and satisfying independent lifestyle.

Furthermore, it presents a range of exceptional services, activities, and unique amenities that elevate the sophisticated lifestyle you already relish.

Lastly, for independent living residents, it ensures assured access to on-site assisted living, memory support, skilled nursing, and rehabilitation services, at minimal additional expense, for as long as the need arises, providing peace of mind and comprehensive care.
