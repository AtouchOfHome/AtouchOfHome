Who we are

Welcome to A Touch of Home, where compassionate care meets a warm and supportive community. At A Touch of Home, we understand that the decision to entrust the care of a loved one to others is one of life's most significant and deeply personal choices. That's why we strive to create an environment that feels like home, where residents are cherished, respected, and surrounded by a team dedicated to their well-being.

Our Commitment:

At the heart of A Touch of Home is a commitment to providing exceptional care tailored to each resident's unique needs. Our experienced and compassionate staff members are dedicated to fostering a sense of belonging and ensuring that every individual feels valued and cherished. We believe that promoting independence, dignity, and quality of life is paramount, and we take pride in creating a supportive atmosphere that enhances the physical, emotional, and social well-being of our residents.

Our Home:

Situated in 4301 Crestridge Dr, Round Rock, TX 78681, our care home is designed to be a true home away from home. With comfortable and thoughtfully furnished living spaces, beautiful outdoor areas, and a range of amenities, we strive to create an environment that promotes relaxation, engagement, and a sense of community. Our residents enjoy delicious, nutritionally balanced meals, engaging activities, and personalized care plans designed to meet their unique needs.
